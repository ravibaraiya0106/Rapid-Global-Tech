Sales & Margin Analytics

Turn raw sales data into strategic growth opportunities with our powerful analytics service

Powerful Features

Gross and Net Margin Calculation

Profitability isn't one-size-fits-all. We break down margins at multiple levels:

  • By Brand: Understand which brands contribute most to your bottom line
  • By Part/SKU: Identify high and low performers in your catalog
  • By Customer Group: See profitability across different customer types
  • Gross Margin: Revenue minus COGS
  • Net Margin: True profitability after all costs

Sales Trend Analysis

Beyond static numbers, trends tell the real story:

  • YoY Growth Analysis: Compare against last year's performance
  • MoM Growth Analysis: Spot short-term fluctuations
  • Identify seasonal cycles and growth patterns
  • Build realistic forecasts based on data
  • Spot early indicators of declining sales

Lost Sales Tracking

Every lost sale is a potential long-term customer loss:

  • Track when and where sales are missed
  • Root cause analysis for lost opportunities
  • Customer feedback collection
  • Competitive price analysis
  • Stock availability audits
  • Sales rep activity tracking

Promotion Impact Measurement

Track before vs after effects of promotions:

  • Did promotions drive real incremental sales?
  • Measure margin effects of promotions
  • Identify best-performing products/bundles
  • Detect cannibalization of regular sales
  • Analyze sales volume, revenue, and margin changes

High-Margin Product Identification

Find your most profitable products:

  • Identify "hidden heroes" in your catalog
  • Create upsell and bundle strategies
  • Improve average order value (AOV)
  • Equip sales teams with profitable options
  • Boost overall profitability without volume increases

Intelligent Alerts

Proactively manage sales health:
